## Germination

This function can be used for an initial plotting and analysis of seed germination data, including visualizing germination time-course curves and determining germination speed or rate values for specific germination fractions. The time course data for each treatment will be plotted and can be identified by the Treatment selection options. The Germination Speed Analysis section calculates the times to specific germination percentages for each treatment, or their inverses, the germination rates. The percentages shown are 10, 16, 50, 84 and 90%. The 16 and 84% values are included as they are one standard deviation below and above the median (50%) value. You can also specify whether to use all data points or to remove points where the germination percentage does not increase since the previous observation time (“Cleaned” data). As the model is based on the times to germination of individual seeds, time points when no additional germination occurred are null data points that do not add information but will (negatively) influence the model fitting. Thus, using the “Cleaned” data is recommended for most cases.
